You are a factory worker.
Raw materials get produced during the gameplay, collect the falling materials using a mouse-controlled cart.
Right click on processor to empty the cart and gain points.
Sometimes the processor gets jammed. use the scroll wheel to crank the processor gears.
Click 'O' to toggle shop mode.
Here you can spend earnt points to get upgrades or buy lives.
Your goal is to not let the materials drop to ground.
Be careful about the falling bomb , avoid it at all costs.
Upgrade your cart capacity in the shop to go to higher levels.
